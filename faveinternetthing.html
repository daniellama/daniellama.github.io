fiftythree-pencil

Fiftythree builds tools for mobile creation, such as paper -- an app that enables users to sketch, write, draw, outline, and color on the ipad. In their website, they use a technique called parallax scrolling, where the images and text are revealed when you scroll down the page. They use high quality photography to break apart the components of their new sophisticated tool for the ipad. Parallax scrolling is a common trend, however, Fiftythree used the effect in a cool and different way to showcase their product.



designspiration

Designspiration is a visual discovery tool that you can use to find ideas for all your projects and interests. Designers can share design, architecture, photography and fashion inspiration. They display their images in a gridded structure that adjusts to the grids around them and fit in nicely.



24 hours of happy

24 Hours of Happy is a 24 hour music video of the song "Happy" by Pharrell Williams. It has an interactive clock that seamlessly ties each clip with the audio. The audio is continuous and doesn't break even when you choose a different time of the day. The clip also relates to each part of the song as well.

design milk

Design Milk is a design blog featuring interior design ideas, architecture, modern furniture, home decor, art, style, and technology founded by jaime derringer. Design Milk has a drop down menu feature on their articles/pictures that are linked to different social medias. It toggles a little animation of the drop down menu when the user clicks on it.

builtbybuffalo

Buffalo is a small web design & development agency based in Brighton, UK. Over the last few years, they've made a reputation for building websites. They have a non-traditional grid that has content loaded by parallax/scrolling down.



draw a stickman

Draw a Stickman is a website where you can custom draw your own person and have the drawing interact with the environment it is placed in. It takes in count of what you drew and use it for the storyline.

pap--the passive aggressive password machine

The PaP machine uses minimal design and humor that gives the user a response based off of their password strength. A set amount of key characters/strength of the password sets off a passive aggressive response. To get a strong password, you essentially need to type in a 20+ letter password.

vw sports cars--golf gti

The Golf GTI website uses beautiful interface to help the user learn about the different aspects of the Golf GTI. The designers considered the 360 aspects as well as detailed close ups of the different features of the car. There were seamless transitions and toggle windows that helped display the information.

the capitol--panem

The Capitol is a digital campaign for Panem-a "peaceful and proposerous land" in Hunger Games. It plays as a promotional piece for Mockingjay" Part 1. The site is mostly white and clean, with video broadcasts from President Snow. It also comes with interactive content and is regularly updated.

the vanishing game

The Vanishing Game uses beautiful design and interface to tell the story by William Boyd in an interactive and digital way. It has full screen moving backgrounds and clean typography. The transitions are clean and smooth. It keeps the user engaged with the text without putting it straight in their face.

i remember

I Remember brings awareness to Alzheimer's Disease. If you don't upload a memory, the website disappears. You can upload your own memory via social media or upload a photo, and you can navigate around different memories.

don jon film

This website was made to promote the movie Don Jon, featuring Joseph Gorden Levitt and Scarlett Johansson. I don't care for the content or movie (heard bad things about it), but the feature that I liked about this site was the split screen navigation.

two minute test

This site is a quick interactive quiz on if you have the basic skills to be a teacher. There were a lot of responsive elements that you were able to drag and put somewhere on the page. Turns out I can be a teacher if I wanted to (according to this test).

google santa tracker

Google has developed a fun site that tracks Santa's wearabouts. Each day reveals a different interaction with various Santa's helpers. You have to look around for levers, buttons, switches, etc. to activate a cute interaction.
